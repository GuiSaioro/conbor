AEC-FG

These Acoplamentos.html are for use in machinery where a torsionally rigid torque is required, especially on frequently varying loads and speeds.

AEC-FI

These Acoplamentos.html are for use in application with high dynamic loads. Damping of peak torques and vibrations as well as electrical insulation between motor and gearbox are further reasons for the use of this coupling type.
